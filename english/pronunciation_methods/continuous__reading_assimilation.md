# 連讀技巧之同化現象

音標同化    
----------------------------------------------
- /t/ + /j/ —> /tʃ/ e.g. Nice to meet you.     
- /d/ + /j/ —> /dʒ/ e.g. Would you like some tea?    
- /s/ + /j/ —> /ʃ/ e.g. I will miss you
